About Me

Hi! I'm am a software developer originally from Manchester, England and now based in Vienna, Austria.

I mostly do front-end web development and specialize in JavaScript in general and AngularJS in particular.

I've been making computers do things since I was in my teens, with varying degrees of success.

By day I work on building business and e-commerce applications.

In my free time I like to keep myself busy exploring more exciting aspects of web technologies, such as canvas, animations and the web-audio API. You can see the result of some of my experimentation on the projects page.

If you'd like to get in touch, use the contact form or, for a faster but possibly less thoughtful response, hit me up on twitter.
